Premiere Pro Centering Cropped Media Tool

Trying to center cropped media in Premiere Pro is quite annoying, this tool simplifies things. Created based on this blog post.
Enter your percentages of crop here. Leave blank for 0.
Use tab to move to the next field. Use enter to generate the value. Use escape to return to the first field.


Copy these and put them into Position inputs of your cropped media
